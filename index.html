Details

This geometric and technical sans-serif typeface offers 16 different styles. Best used for display, headlines, logotype, and short body copy paragraphs. The sans serif family includes eight weights ranging from Thin to Ultra with corresponding italics. Designed by Mattox Shuler, the creator of the small font foundry Fort, this typeface has been used by the NFL, Marvel Studios, Sports Illustrated, and more.
